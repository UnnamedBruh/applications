What exactly Is Marniline?

Marniline is a console-based application that may use the Command Prompt's console, which is the program's UI interface. The application is designed to access your disk, and others may not be implemented. You should use this application at your own risk, since this application may have bugs that need to be addressed. By downloading the application, you agree to make sure not to open any internal files created automatically on your disk drive in your computer, and do so only to and if a copy of an internal file has been created only by you or someone else (and not the internals itself) or the internal files are opened from an emulated environment. FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q: Is the program secure to download?

Short A: Yes.

Long A: Yes, the program may access your disk, and only the file you had loaded. However, it is recommended you make copies of the files you want to load into the program before changing the files; the program may completely overwrite the file you loaded. There is no redo and undo feature.

Q: Are we (the users who created the application, published the application and made updates to the application) responsible for the damages from the application?

A: No; you (the user/person downloading the application) are legally held responsible for the damages you cause on your own computer from using the application. The application is only functional if you interact with it. We will also highly recommend you to never edit the original internal files of your computer, as doing so will cause your computer irrepairable damage, or cause minor or major issues with your computer. However if you know the risks of this application, then you may experiment with the internal files - but make a copy of them ONTO ANOTHER FOLDER, not the internal files folder themselves!

Q: Is this application free to download?
